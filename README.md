# 🌾 FarmConnect

## Business Analyst Case Study

---

## 📌 Project Overview

FarmConnect is a Business Analyst portfolio project that aims to bridge the gap between farmers and buyers through a digital marketplace. The platform enables farmers to list agricultural products, participate in auctions, receive real-time market prices, and connect directly with buyers without unnecessary intermediaries.

This project demonstrates the complete Business Analysis lifecycle, including requirement gathering, stakeholder analysis, business process modeling, functional requirements, business rules, and solution design.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

Many farmers struggle to sell their produce at fair prices due to limited market access, price uncertainty, and dependence on intermediaries. Buyers also face challenges in finding verified farmers and transparent pricing.

FarmConnect addresses these issues by providing a centralized digital platform for direct farmer-to-buyer interaction.

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

- Connect farmers directly with buyers.
- Reduce dependency on intermediaries.
- Increase pricing transparency.
- Enable online agricultural product auctions.
- Provide real-time market price information.
- Support secure farmer verification.
- Improve the overall agricultural trading experience.
